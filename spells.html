AI Medium (@arbhot) is an interactive Twitterbot powered by Stable Diffusion. Construct your incantation using the following structure:

@artbhot #conjure <your spell here>

After one minute synthesyzing another world, the AI medium will reply with your vision. Here are some examples:

@artbhot #conjure a vision down the rabbit hole of halloweentown wonderland, creepy, apocalyptic

@artbhot #conjure a vision of a machine graveyard from the Unseen World, cyberpunk, futurist

artbhot #conjure a vision of flaming Balrog demon in a haunted graveyard, grim, bleak

Please note: Maximum characters for the tweet text is currently 200. Do not use @artbhot for any NSFW content. Obscene and offensive tweets will be removed. More information on @artbhot can be found here.
