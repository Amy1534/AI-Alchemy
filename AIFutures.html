AI Futures Panel: Creative AI Systems

This panel has been put together with the intent of exploring the rapidly growing discussion around the use of creative AI systems. In the past few years, media created by image and video synthesis deep learning algorithms has been flooding the internet like never before, and advancements in this field are occuring at an unprecedented rate.

The thrust of this event will be to explore what we can gain from such systems, as well as what we should be wary of moving forward in to this technological space. We will explore ideas about where we can attribute creative responsibility in the generative process, and also the impact this may have on society.

We plan for this to be a black mirror / white mirror approach, exploring the best and the worst of this technology but with a mutual interest in steering the field in a positive direction for the future.
